Homestead Robotics

FRC Team 670

We are a high school robotics team based in Cupertino, California that is dedicated to spread knowledge and encourage interest in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Maths). Every year, Homestead Robotics competes in the international FIRST Robotics Competition as Team 670.

More about us

Meet the team

    What We Do  As students of Homestead High School who are passionate about STEM, our goal is to foster STEM interest among both the students and our community through competition, education, and outreach.

Education Our officer team and mentors
organize workshops open to all students.
We teach CAD, programming, and fabrication
skills in a hands-on environment. 

COMPETITION         Since 2001, we've competed in the FIRST Robotics Challenge. In 2018, Team 670 placed 4th at the Utah Regional, finishing on the 2nd place alliance, and achieving 7th at the Silicon Valley Regional.    

Outreach  One of Team 670's major goals is to promote STEM education and interest, both within Homestead High School and in the wider community. We host demonstrations at community events, schools, and libraries, educating young students about technology, engineering, and teamwork.
